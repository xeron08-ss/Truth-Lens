🧠 Deepfake Awareness Hub

Understanding the deepfake landscape — how AI-generated media works and how to spot it.

👁️
Visual Artifacts
Look for unnatural skin smoothness, ear inconsistencies, hair boundary blurring, and lighting mismatches.
🎭
Face Swap Tells
GAN-generated faces often show inconsistent blinking, asymmetric eye specular reflections, and edge artifacts.
🌊
Diffusion Patterns
Stable Diffusion and DALL·E images show characteristic noise patterns detectable in frequency domain.
🎙️
Voice Cloning
AI-cloned voices lack natural breath patterns, show compression artifacts, and have unnatural prosody.
📱
Social Media Risk
AI-generated content spreads 6× faster than corrections. Always verify before sharing sensitive media.
